 **Fruit Ripeness Evaluation**: The API will assess the color, texture, and other surface characteristics of specific fruits to determine their ripeness stage (Ripe, Rotten, Unripe, or Overripe).

 **Target Fruits**:  This functionality is designed explicitly for specific tropical and temperate fruits that are common nutritional fruit crops, namely,
 Lakatan Bananas, Carabao Mangoes, Red Apples, Cantaloupe Melons, D'Anjou Pears, Oranges, Peaches, and Concord Grapes.
